#+title: Overfitting


The error on training data is called _training error_.
The error on test data is called _test error_.


Overfitting occurs when the gap between the training error and test error is too large.

Overfitting can be caused by high [[id:4487C2B2-FB61-47B9-9315-DEC565AABA75][Capacity]] and [[id:B0ECD4D7-94CF-40E9-AAA8-ECF77C6E9A31][Lack of Data]].
