#+title: ZMK

https://zmk.dev/docs



ZMK (Zephy MIT Keybaord) Firmware is an open source (MIT) keyboard firmware built on the [[https://zephyrproject.org/][Zephyr Project]] Real Time Operating System (RTOS).
ZMK's goal is to provide a modern, wireless, and powerful firmware free of licensing issues.
