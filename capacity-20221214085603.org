#+title: Capacity

[[id:94DA8AD3-43D1-4357-9FAF-46F820106FD7][Overfitting]]

[[id:C2ECD2E8-9849-453E-AE09-09C2C5384535][Underfitting]]



We can control whether a model is more likely to overfit ([[id:94DA8AD3-43D1-4357-9FAF-46F820106FD7][Overfitting]]) or underfit ([[id:C2ECD2E8-9849-453E-AE09-09C2C5384535][Underfitting]]) by altering its *capacity*.
The capacity is the pattern space (family of functions) we can learn from.
