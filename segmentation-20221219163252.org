#+title: Segmentation

[[id:4ACC20C2-B350-4907-BED4-6A5928C288FF][FCN]]

Segmentation is the classification on pixel level.
It usually have the following types:
- semantic segmentation
- instance segmentation
- panoptic segmentation (semantic segmentation + instance segmentation)
