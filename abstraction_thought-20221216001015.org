#+title: Abstraction Thought

Abstraction thought includes the following cases:
- we extract something import in common things.
- we use something that does not exist to replace the existing thing.

[[id:05A1E69D-6775-45FC-91D1-B8F013023B53][Concretize Thought]] is the opposite of the abstraction thought. 

This is thought is often used in [[id:CC08C7CD-063F-4069-9FA5-ADD61A9F8771][Math]].
